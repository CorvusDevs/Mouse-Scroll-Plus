Any mouse, trackpad-smooth.

Mouse Scroll Plus gives your mouse fluid, glide-based scrolling, plus reverse, speed and momentum tuning, button remapping, and per-app rules.

For macOS 13 and later. Coming soon to the Mac App Store.

Built for how you scroll

Small, focused, and entirely on your Mac.

Smooth scrolling

Turn each wheel notch into a fluid glide instead of a stepped jump. Tune speed, smoothness, and acceleration.

Reverse and tune

Flip the wheel direction while your trackpad stays untouched. Add momentum and dial in exactly how it feels.

Remap extra buttons

Assign shortcuts, media keys, and actions to side buttons. Left, right, and the wheel are never changed.

Per-app rules

Turn the effect off in the apps you choose, or give an app its own scrolling. Set a shortcut to toggle it anywhere.

Middle-button autoscroll

Hold the middle button and move to scroll, Windows-style, or hold a key to pause the effect for precise work.

Private and lightweight

No accounts, no tracking, no network. Everything runs on your Mac in a tiny, sandboxed app.

Everything it does

Reverse direction

Flip vertical or horizontal scrolling independently. The trackpad is never touched.

Speed & smoothness

Tune how far each notch travels and how long the glide lasts.

Acceleration

Fast flicks travel farther, like a trackpad, with adjustable strength.

Momentum

A quick scroll keeps drifting for a moment after you let go.

Horizontal scroll

Hold a key to scroll sideways instead of up and down.

Hold to pause

Hold a key for raw, precise scrolling whenever you need it.

System & media shortcuts

Map extra buttons to shortcuts, media keys, and editing commands.

Open apps, URLs & Shortcuts

Bind a button to launch an app, open a link, or run a Shortcut.

Per-modifier layers

Give the same button a different action when you hold a modifier.

Global toggle shortcut

Flip everything on or off from anywhere with a hotkey.

Names your mouse

Detects your mouse and lets you give it a friendly name.

Launch at login

Runs quietly in the menu bar, ready the moment you log in.

Stepped mode

Prefer classic stepped scrolling? Set how many lines each notch moves.
